VMware VCP-NV 2022 Certified

Published on Feb 28, 2022
Updated on Feb 28, 2022

Finally I got the VMware VCP-NV 2022 Certification, taking the exam 2V0-41.20.

I came from 5+ years experience in virtualization area, working with VMware, Proxmox and so on.
Experience on vSphere environments is not a plus, but a mandatory requirement: a lot of questions cover topics not only related to NSX-T but also to vSphere (ESXi, vCenter, etc.), so before taking the exam please be sure to have experience or to have lot of home labs.

During the course VMware NSX-T Data Center: Install, Configure, Manage [V3.0] I wrote a lot of notes, tips, more-indeep topics and so on.
I’m reorganizing them in order to create a Note study guide: it will be released next week.
