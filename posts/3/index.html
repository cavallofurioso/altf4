VMware VCP-NV 2022 - 2V0-41.20 NSX-T Study Guide

Published on Feb 28, 2022
Updated on Jun 1, 2022

Finally I got the VMware VCP-NV 2022 Certification, taking the exam 2V0-41.20.

I came from 5+ years experience in virtualization area, working with VMware, Proxmox and so on.
Experiences on vSphere environments are not a plus but a mandatory requirement: a lot of questions cover topics not only related to NSX-T but also to vSphere (ESXi, vCenter, etc.) so before taking the exam please be sure to have experience or to have lots of home labs where you are not a dragon.

During the course VMware NSX-T Data Center: Install, Configure, Manage [V3.0] I wrote a lot of notes, tips, more-indeep topics and so on.
I have organized them in order to create a Note Study Guide.

  1. Ep.1 - Intro - Architecture
  2. Ep.2 - Management Plane - Control Plane - Data Plane
  3. Ep.3 - Logical Switching - Logical Routing
  4. Ep.4 - VRF-Lite - EVPN - Multicast
  5. Ep.5 - Logical Bridging - Security
  6. Ep.6 - Monitoring - Authentication/Authorization - Services - VPN - Federation
