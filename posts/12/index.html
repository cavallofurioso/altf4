How Bluetooth has landed on our devices?

Someone can think that some advanced technologies available today in 2022 have a specific history beside, a specific market study, etc.
A wireless technology that has been available first on laptop and next on smartphone was Bluetooth.

Can u imagine that is was born during a beer drink? It’s amazing.
Everything started in 1997 when IBM Product R&D Adalio Sanchez met his neighbor Nils Rydbeck, Ericsson Mobile CTO.
During their chatting Sanchez said that they wanted to improve their ThinkPad laptop, implementing a way to provide wireless internet connectivity (so no need to plug it into wall plug).
The bad news was at IBM they have just pushed down this project, just because it required lot of money for research and lot of laptop battery in order to be used.

So then Nils Ericsson CTO explained that they were implemented a technology that provide a short-wave technology solution.
The idea was to add it to a laptop and also to a mobile phone: the last one will be connected to internet using mobile line and then bluetooth connection (between phone and laptop) will allow laptop to be connected to the web wirelessly (without a battery waste).

First consumer devices with Bluetooth landed in 2001: Ericsson T39 and ThinkPad A30.
